# Privacy Policy for CleanShot for YouTube

**Last Updated:** February 10, 2026

**CleanShot for YouTube** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. User-Generated Content
We collect the data you explicitly generate while using the extension, including:
* Screenshots captured from YouTube videos.
* Timestamps and video titles.
* Notes and text you input into the side panel.

### B. No Personal Data Collection
We **do not** collect personal identification information (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers). We do not track your browsing history outside of the specific YouTube video pages where you activate the extension.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the functionality of the extension:
* **To Display:** To show your captured images and notes in the side panel.
* **To Export:** To allow you to copy images to the clipboard, save them to your computer, or generate PDF reports.
* **To Generate Prompts:** To structure your notes into a format suitable for AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT) upon your request.

## 3. Data Storage (Local Only)

**All data is stored locally on your device.**
* We use the Chrome `storage.local` API to save your screenshots and notes.
* Your data **never leaves your browser** and is **never transmitted to external servers** managed by us.
* If you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data, your saved notes and screenshots will be permanently deleted.

## 4. Third-Party Services

### A. YouTube
This extension interacts with the YouTube website to capture video frames and timestamps. We are not affiliated with YouTube or Google.

### B. Lemon Squeezy (Payments)
If you choose to support us via the "Buy me a coffee" button, you will be redirected to **Lemon Squeezy**. All payment processing is handled by Lemon Squeezy, and their privacy policy governs the transaction. We do not have access to your credit card information.

### C. AI Tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude)
Our "Copy AI Prompt" feature formats your data into text and copies it to your clipboard. **We do not send your data directly to any AI API.** You voluntarily paste the copied text into the AI tool of your choice.

## 5. Permissions

We request the minimum permissions necessary:
* **`activeTab`**: To capture the visible tab when you click the "Clean Shot" button.
* **`storage`**: To save your work locally so it persists after a page refresh.
* **`sidePanel`**: To display the user interface.
* **Host Permissions (`*://*.youtube.com/*`)**: To enable functionality specifically on YouTube pages.

## 6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally:
* **Access & Deletion**: You can view all stored data within the extension's side panel. You can delete specific items or use the "Trash" (Reset) button to delete all data instantly.
* **Export**: You can copy or download your data at any time using the provided buttons.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through the extension update notes or the Chrome Web Store listing.
