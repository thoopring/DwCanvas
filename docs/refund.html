Refund Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

TL;DR: Try it free first. If you pay and it doesn't work as described, we'll refund you within 7 days. No questions asked.

1. Free Trial

CleanShot offers a free trial that lets you generate 1 carousel from any YouTube video. No credit card required. We encourage you to try the free trial before purchasing a subscription to make sure the Service meets your needs.

2. Refund Window

  • 7-day refund: If you subscribe and are not satisfied, request a refund within 7 days of your first payment. We will issue a full refund, no questions asked.
  • After 7 days: Refunds are granted on a case-by-case basis for technical issues that prevent you from using the Service.

3. How to Request a Refund

Email thoopring@gmail.com with your account email and the reason for your request. We aim to process refunds within 3 business days.

4. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time through the LemonSqueezy customer portal. After cancellation:

  • Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  • Your library data remains accessible in read-only mode.
  • No further charges will be made.

5. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Requests made more than 30 days after payment.
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations.
  • Dissatisfaction with AI output quality after the free trial (you had the chance to evaluate for free).
