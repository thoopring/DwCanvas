Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

CleanShot ("we," "our," or "us") operates a Chrome browser extension that helps users create LinkedIn carousel posts from YouTube video content. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This is used solely for authentication and account management.

B. Video Transcripts

When you use the "Generate Carousel" feature, the extension extracts caption text from the YouTube video you are watching. This transcript is sent to our server for AI processing and is stored in your account library.

C. Generated Content

AI-generated carousel content (slides, hooks, CTAs) is stored in your account so you can access it later from the Library.

D. Payment Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by LemonSqueezy, our Merchant of Record. We do not store or have access to your credit card details. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy governs payment transactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To identify your account and manage your subscription.
  • AI Processing: Transcripts are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate carousel content. Transcripts are not used for AI training.
  • Service Improvement: Aggregate, anonymized usage statistics (e.g., number of carousels generated) may be used to improve the service.

3. Data Storage

  • Account data and generated content are stored on Cloudflare infrastructure (D1 database, R2 storage).
  • Data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • If you cancel your subscription, your library data becomes read-only but is not deleted. You can request full deletion by contacting us.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Video transcripts are sent for processing. Anthropic does not use API inputs for training.
  • LemonSqueezy: For payment processing.
  • Cloudflare: For hosting and infrastructure.

5. Browser Permissions

  • activeTab: To interact with the YouTube page you are viewing.
  • storage: To cache your session locally.
  • identity: For Google sign-in.
  • sidePanel: To display the CleanShot interface.
  • Host permission (youtube.com): To extract captions from YouTube videos.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data in the Library tab.
  • Deletion: Contact us at thoopring@gmail.com to request full account deletion.
  • Export: Download your carousel content via the ZIP export feature.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries: thoopring@gmail.com
